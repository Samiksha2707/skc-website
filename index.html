About the Event

Get ready for the most thrilling soccer event of the year! The The Soccer Knockout Challenge (SKC) is a premier inter-collegiate football tournament organized by NCRD Sterling Institute of Management Studies. Now in its Fifth season, SKC has become one of the most anticipated sporting events in the region, attracting skilled footballers from colleges across Navi Mumbai, Mumbai, Thane.
